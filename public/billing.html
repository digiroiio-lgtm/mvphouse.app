Billing & Subscription Policy

Last Updated: 01.01.2026

1. Introduction

This Billing & Subscription Policy explains how billing, renewals, pricing, and cancellations operate across all MVP HOUSE LTD (“we”, “us”, “our”) SaaS and micro-SaaS products. By purchasing or using any subscription, you agree to these terms.

2. Payment Processor

All payments are securely processed by Stripe. We do not store or access your full card information at any time.

3. Subscription Renewals

All subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing cycle unless cancelled beforehand. The renewal charge will be applied to the payment method on file.

4. Pricing and Changes

We reserve the right to modify pricing. Users will be notified at least 7 days before any pricing change takes effect.

5. Free Trials

If a plan includes a free trial, no charges will be applied until the trial period ends. To avoid charges, cancel before the trial expiration. Once the trial converts to a paid plan, billing will follow the standard subscription cycle.

6. Billing Cycles

Subscriptions may be billed monthly, quarterly, annually, or via custom enterprise terms. The billing frequency is specified during checkout.

7. Failed Payments

If a payment fails, Stripe will automatically attempt to process the charge again.
If the payment method continues to fail:

  • Your account may be temporarily suspended.
  • You will receive notification to update your payment method.
  • Loss of access continues until successful payment.

8. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your user dashboard or by contacting us at support@mvphouse.app.

Cancellation applies only to future billing cycles. We do not issue refunds for the remaining time in the current billing period.

9. One-Time Purchases

Some products may be offered as a one-time payment. These products do not renew and are non-refundable once purchased and delivered.

10. Taxes

Depending on your location, VAT or applicable taxes may be added to the total amount charged.

11. Chargebacks

Any chargeback filed without contacting us first may result in account suspension.
We encourage users to reach out for support before initiating bank disputes.

12. Contact Information

For billing and subscription questions:
Email: support@mvphouse.app
Phone Support: Provided for B2B customers only.
