Last Updated: 01.01.2026

By accessing the Services, you agree to comply fully with this AUP.

1. Prohibited Activities

Users must not engage in any activity that violates law, harms the platform, or interferes with other users. Prohibited activities include, but are not limited to:

1.1 Illegal Activity

  • Any action that violates local, national, or international laws.
  • Distribution of illegal content.
  • Unlawful surveillance or data collection.

1.2 Fraud & Abuse

  • Impersonation of any person or entity.
  • Using stolen payment methods.
  • Creating fake accounts.
  • Submitting fraudulent chargebacks.

1.3 Spam & Unsolicited Activity

  • Bulk messaging or spam campaigns.
  • Automated scraping or harvesting of user data.
  • Sending harmful, deceptive, or misleading content.

1.4 Security Violations

  • Attempting to bypass authentication or security systems.
  • Probing, scanning, or testing the vulnerability of the platform.
  • Reverse engineering or attempting to extract source code.
  • Deploying malware, bots, viruses, or harmful scripts.

1.5 Resource Misuse

  • Running automated scripts to overload systems.
  • Circumventing API limits or Fair Use restrictions.
  • Excessive or abusive consumption of AI or compute resources.

2. AI-Specific Prohibited Uses

If you use any AI-powered Service, you must not generate or attempt to generate:

  • Illegal content or instructions for illegal activity

Users are fully responsible for verifying the accuracy of AI outputs.
